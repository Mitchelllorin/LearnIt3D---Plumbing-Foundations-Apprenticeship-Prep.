- A) Apprentice (4th year)
- B) Journeyman plumber
- C) Master plumber
- D) OSHA Competent Person

**3.** A RIDGID 300 threading machine is most commonly used to cut NPT threads on which pipe material?

- A) PEX-A (cross-linked polyethylene)
- B) Type L copper tubing

- D) ABS drain pipe

**4.** Which pressing system is used for joining carbon steel (black iron) pipe without threading, using a hydraulic press tool and a pre-installed sleeve fitting?

- A) Viega ProPress (copper press)

- D) ASTM F1960 expansion connection

**5.** For a given nominal copper pipe size, which statement about outside diameter (OD) is ALWAYS true regardless of Type (K, L, or M)?

- C) The OD is always 1/8" larger than the nominal size, and is the same for all types
- D) The OD varies by manufacturer
