## Questions

**1.** The word "plumbing" is derived from the Latin word for which element?

- A) Copper
- B) Iron
- C) Lead
- D) Tin

---

**2.** Which public health engineer is most closely associated with designing London's intercepting sewer system, completed in 1875?

- A) John Snow
- B) Joseph Bazalgette
- C) Edwin Chadwick
- D) Thomas Crapper

---

**3.** A plumber who has completed a UA apprenticeship program and passed a state licensing exam — but cannot pull permits or run a contracting business — holds which license level?

- A) Apprentice
- B) Journeyman
- C) Master
- D) Specialty Technician

---

**4.** Which organization publishes the **Uniform Plumbing Code (UPC)**?

- A) ICC (International Code Council)
- B) ASPE (American Society of Plumbing Engineers)
- C) IAPMO (International Association of Plumbing and Mechanical Officials)
- D) PHCC (Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors Association)

---

**5.** Sewer gas is primarily a mixture of which gases that make it both toxic AND potentially flammable?

- A) Carbon dioxide and nitrogen
- B) Methane and hydrogen sulfide
- C) Carbon monoxide and oxygen
- D) Ammonia and chlorine

---

**6.** After cutting copper tubing with a wheel cutter, which step MUST be performed before inserting the tube into a fitting?

- A) Apply PTFE tape to the tube OD
- B) Apply flux to the tube OD only
- C) Remove the internal burr with a deburring tool
- D) Heat the tube end with a torch to anneal it

---

**7.** Which ASTM standard governs the PEX crimp connection method using copper crimp rings?

- A) ASTM F1960
- B) ASTM F1807
- C) ASTM F2098
- D) ASTM D2846

---

**8.** When using a propane torch to solder a copper joint, the heat should be applied to:

- A) The solder wire directly, to pre-melt it before applying to the joint
- B) The pipe body, at least 6 inches from the fitting
- C) The fitting body, so that the fitting heats the pipe
- D) Both the pipe and the fitting equally at the same time

---

**9.** A pipe wrench should NEVER be used on which of the following pipe types?

- A) Black iron threaded pipe
- B) Galvanized steel threaded pipe
- C) Chrome-plated supply tube
- D) 3-inch cast iron hubless pipe

---

**10.** Which copper tube type has the thickest wall and is most commonly specified for underground water service lines?

- A) Type M
- B) Type L
- C) Type DWV
- D) Type K

---

**11.** CPVC pipe uses which outside diameter sizing system?

- A) IPS (Iron Pipe Size) — same as Schedule 40 PVC
- B) CTS (Copper Tube Size) — same as copper tubing
- C) NPS (Nominal Pipe Size) — half-inch increments
- D) DN (Diameter Nominal) — metric only

---

**12.** A sanitary tee (standard pattern) is only approved in which orientation?

- A) Horizontal branch to horizontal run (any direction)
- B) Any orientation, as long as the pipe is 3" or smaller
- C) Horizontal branch connecting to a vertical drain stack
- D) Underground drain runs only

---

**13.** What device must be installed to prevent galvanic corrosion when connecting copper pipe directly to a galvanized steel fitting?

- A) Ball valve
- B) Gate valve
- C) Check valve
- D) Dielectric union

---

**14.** According to the IPC, a pressure-reducing valve (PRV) is required on the building water supply when the static pressure exceeds:

- A) 60 psi
- B) 80 psi
- C) 100 psi
- D) 120 psi

---

**15.** What is the maximum recommended velocity for a cold water supply pipe to avoid erosion corrosion in copper?

- A) 5 ft/s
- B) 8 ft/s
- C) 12 ft/s
- D) 15 ft/s

---

**16.** Which backflow preventer type is required for HIGH-HAZARD cross-connections such as boiler fill lines and chemical-injection systems?

- A) Atmospheric vacuum breaker (AVB)
- B) Pressure vacuum breaker (PVB)
- C) Double check valve assembly (DCVA)
- D) Reduced-pressure zone (RPZ) device

---

**17.** When is a thermal expansion tank required on the cold-water supply feeding a residential water heater?

- A) Whenever the water heater is a gas appliance
- B) Whenever a PRV, check valve, or backflow preventer creates a closed system on the cold supply side
- C) Whenever the supply pressure exceeds 60 psi
- D) Whenever the water heater exceeds 50 gallons in capacity

---

**18.** The Temperature and Pressure Relief (T&P) valve on a water heater must be piped to discharge within how many inches of the floor (or other safe point)?

- A) 2 inches
- B) 4 inches
- C) 6 inches
- D) 12 inches

---

**19.** What is the purpose of the **dip tube** inside a storage-tank water heater?

- A) To conduct combustion gases to the flue
- B) To deliver incoming cold water to the bottom of the tank so cold water doesn't mix with hot water at the outlet
- C) To protect the tank lining from corrosion by sacrificial oxidation
- D) To bypass the thermostat during a high-temperature emergency

---

**20.** A PEX expansion fitting system (ASTM F1960) uses which tool — distinct from the crimp system — to prepare the pipe end before inserting the fitting?

- A) PEX crimp tool with copper ring
- B) Stainless clamp tool
- C) Expansion tool that enlarges the pipe end and ring before the fitting is inserted
- D) Standard compression fitting tool
